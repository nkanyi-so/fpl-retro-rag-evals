Erling Haaland is the other elite option: lower-owned at this stage (around 17%) but with the kindest fixture on paper of the premium forwards — Manchester City travel to Wolves, a tie rated the lowest difficulty among the big captaincy candidates. The choice is the safe, popular home pick (Salah) against the easier-fixture differential (Haaland away at Wolves).
