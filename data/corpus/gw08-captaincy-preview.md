GW8 is a template-versus-differential captaincy call, but the template is hard to oppose. Erling Haaland leads all players on points-to-date (70), is the form pick (46 points across the last four GWs), and is now owned by around 60% of squads — and Manchester City are at home to Everton. The case for a differential is weak this week: the obvious alternative, City team-mate Antoine Semenyo, shares the same fixture but has himself climbed to near-template ownership (around 58%), so captaining him is not a real differential. A genuine lower-owned punt would be Mohamed Kudus (around 33% owned) at home to Aston Villa. On the pre-deadline numbers, the safe template armband on Haaland is the stronger play and the differential case does not clearly beat it.
